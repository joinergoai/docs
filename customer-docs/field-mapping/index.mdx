Use these articles when you are setting up CRM properties, pipeline stages, company and domain context, meeting-title classification, advanced CRM controls, or backfill expectations.
## Who this section is for

- RevOps, sales operations, CRM owners, and admins with CRM permissions.

## Before you start

- Use a CRM account with the read and write permissions required for the fields and stages you want Ergo to use.
- Map deal, contact, company, pipeline, and stage data before enabling broad CRM automation.
- Test one representative record before rolling out settings broadly.
- Recheck mappings after CRM admins rename, remove, reorder, or restrict fields or stages.
