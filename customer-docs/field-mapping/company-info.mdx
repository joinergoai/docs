Use Company Info to give Ergo durable context about your own company, product, and positioning. This context can help summaries, drafts, and AI-assisted reasoning, but it should stay factual and stable.

## Who can use this

- RevOps, sales operations, CRM owners, and admins with CRM permissions.

## Before you start

- Use a CRM account that can read and update the fields Ergo needs.
- Map properties, pipelines, and stages before enabling broad CRM automation.
- Test changes on one record before rolling them out.

## Steps

- Add or review your company name, description, and solution context.
- Keep language factual, current, and durable.
- Avoid customer-specific promises or temporary launch language.
- Update the context when positioning, ICP, or packaging changes.

## What to expect

- Company Info supports AI context; it does not create CRM fields by itself.
- Overly broad descriptions can make drafts and summaries sound generic.
- Outdated positioning can affect AI-generated language.

## Common issues

- Company context is stale after a positioning change.
- The description includes private customer examples.
- The description is too vague to guide AI outputs.
