Start with the article that matches the setup owner, source, or workflow in front of you.
## Who this section is for

- Admins, RevOps teams, operators, managers, and users completing first-time setup.

## Before you start

- Decide who owns Ergo account setup before inviting the broader team.
- Connect organization-level systems before asking users to complete personal setup.
- Use role-specific setup pages when someone only needs the part of the rollout path they own.
- Confirm admin ownership for gated areas such as pricing, reporting, CRM fields, and organization defaults.
