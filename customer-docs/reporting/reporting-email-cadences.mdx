Reporting email cadences send recurring summaries based on selected Reporting fields and analysis instructions.

## Who can use this

- Reporting email cadences are admin-only in the Reporting page.
- Recipients can include organization members and external email addresses.

## Create a cadence

1. Open **Reporting**.
2. Select **Email Cadences**.
3. Select **New Cadence**.
4. In **Setup**, enter a cadence name, weekly send day, send time, timezone, and enabled state.
5. In **Recipients**, choose organization members or add external email addresses.
6. In **Fields**, choose manual field selection or auto field selection.
7. In **Analysis**, add instructions for what the report should emphasize.
8. Save the cadence.

New cadences can be saved as disabled drafts. When you continue to the analysis step for a new cadence, Ergo defaults the cadence to enabled unless you turn it off.

## Field selection modes

- **Manual**: select up to 10 available Ergo reporting fields or rubric fields.
- **Auto**: leave selected fields empty and let Ergo choose fields for the run.

Manual mode requires at least one field before the cadence can be enabled. Remove unavailable fields before saving.

## Recipients and schedule

- Recipient emails must be valid and cannot be duplicated.
- A pending external email must be added or cleared before saving.
- Cadences use a weekly schedule.
- The timezone must be a valid IANA timezone.

## Test and send now

Use **test run** before enabling a cadence or after major field changes. Use **send now** when you want to queue an immediate send for the cadence's configured recipients.

Both actions enqueue a reporting email run. A queued action is not the same as confirmed delivery; check run history for the final result.
