Use this page to manage Desktop settings after the app is installed and signed in.

## Who can use this

- Desktop users maintaining their local app setup.
- Admins and operators troubleshooting stale app versions or settings.

## Before you start

- Confirm the app is signed in as the expected user.
- Keep the app open long enough for settings and update checks to complete.
- Review whether your workspace uses auto-record detected meetings.

## Steps

- Open Desktop settings.
- Confirm the authenticated user.
- Review recording and capture-related settings.
- If your team uses auto-recording, confirm the desktop notetaker configuration allows detected-meeting recording.
- Check whether a newer app version is available through the approved update/download path.
- If the in-app update path is unavailable or blocked, download the current app again from the approved Ergo download path.
- Sign out only when you are ready to clear the local authenticated session.

## What to expect

- The app can restore and refresh WorkOS authentication from local session data.
- Desktop notetaker configuration includes a setting for auto-recording detected meetings.
- The app includes an updater path, but workspace access and release availability determine what users can download or install.

## Common issues

- The app is signed in as the wrong user.
- Auto-record is disabled even though manual recording works.
- A managed device blocks updates or app permissions.
- Signing out clears local authentication and requires browser sign-in again.
