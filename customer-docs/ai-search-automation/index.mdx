Use this section when you need Ergo to answer questions, search across indexed context, work with deal or meeting context, or run recurring agent prompts.

AI answers and automations depend on connected sources, permissions, and the freshness of indexed data. Review sources, proposed actions, and delivery results before using AI output as a customer-facing or CRM-changing decision.

## Who this section is for

- Reps and managers asking questions about deals, meetings, emails, and documents.
- RevOps teams setting up scheduled agent runs and delivery destinations.
- Admins troubleshooting search, source access, or run history issues.

## Before you start

- Connect the sources you expect Ergo to use, such as meetings, email, CRM, Slack, or uploaded documents.
- Confirm the user has access to the records they expect to search or ask about.
- Treat AI answers as summaries or proposed actions until you review the source context.
