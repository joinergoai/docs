Use this page when a member should stay in the organization but needs a different team role or seat type. Role changes are separate from team moves, meeting access grants, and reporting access grants.

## Who can use this

- Super admins can promote team members, demote secondary admins, and convert users between standard-user and spectator seats.
- Primary and secondary admins can review role assignments, but role changes are guarded by super-admin permissions.

## Before you start

- Confirm the person is on the correct team. If not, use [Add/remove/move members](./add-remove-move-members) first.
- Decide whether you are changing admin authority or converting between a standard user and spectator.
- Review any separate [meeting and reporting access](./grant-meeting-reporting-access) grants that should change after the role update.
- Ask the member to expect a refresh or reconnect step after spectator conversion.

## Promote a member to secondary admin

Use **Promote to Admin** for a regular team member who should become a secondary admin.

1. Open **Admin** and choose the team.
2. Open the member's action menu.
3. Select **Promote to Admin**.
4. Confirm the promotion.

Ergo moves the person from **Members** to **Secondary Admins** for that team and updates their organization and dashboard role to admin.

## Demote a secondary admin

Use **Demote to User** when a secondary admin should remain on the team but no longer have admin privileges.

1. Open the secondary admin's action menu.
2. Select **Demote to User**.
3. Confirm the demotion.

Ergo moves the person from **Secondary Admins** to **Members** for that team and updates their organization and dashboard role to user.

## Convert a standard user or spectator

Use **Convert to Spectator** or **Convert to User** when the person's seat type should change.

When converting a standard user to spectator, Ergo:

- Marks the dashboard as a spectator seat.
- Disconnects supported third-party notetakers and Slack for that user.
- Turns off email monitoring, follow-up email, external email client, and surfacing settings.
- Disables Ergo for that user.
- Disconnects the user's Recall calendar connection when one exists.

When converting a spectator back to a standard user, Ergo changes the spectator flag back to standard user. The user may need to reconnect integrations, re-enable email settings, and re-enable Ergo afterward.

Primary admins cannot be converted with this action.

## What to expect

- Role changes update the Admin member table after the data refreshes.
- Promotion and demotion change admin authority; they do not automatically change separate meeting/reporting grants.
- Spectator conversion intentionally disconnects or disables automation-related settings so the seat becomes view-oriented.
- Converting back to standard user does not automatically restore every disconnected integration or toggle.

## Common issues

- **The action is missing.** Promote appears for members, demote appears for secondary admins, and convert appears only when the current user is allowed to perform the conversion.
- **The primary admin needs to change.** Use the team primary-admin reassignment flow instead of convert, promote, or demote.
- **The person still cannot see meetings or reports.** Check meeting access and reporting access separately.
- **Integrations are disconnected after spectator conversion.** This is expected. Reconnect and re-enable the relevant settings after converting back to standard user.
