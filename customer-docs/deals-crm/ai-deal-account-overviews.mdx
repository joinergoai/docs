Use AI overviews to get oriented quickly on a deal or company. They are only as good as the available CRM, meeting, email, and connected activity context, so review the underlying sources before sending a message or updating the CRM.

## Who can use this

- Sales reps, account owners, managers, RevOps, and admins working from pipeline and account context.

## Before you start

- Connect CRM and complete field mapping before relying on CRM writeback.
- Check pipeline, view, filter, ownership, and record matching before assuming a record is missing.
- Confirm Deals access is enabled for your workspace; contact your admin or Ergo support if you do not see it.

## Steps

- Open the deal or company detail page.
- Read the overview alongside health, activity, emails, meetings, and CRM fields.
- Check activity, email, meeting, CRM, and connected-source sections for the evidence behind important claims.
- Ask follow-up questions only when you have confirmed the context is current enough.

## What to expect

- Deal and company overviews can be generated at a point in time and may not reflect activity that Ergo has not indexed yet.
- Overview quality depends on source availability and record matching.
- Overviews should not be treated as complete or final without source review.

## Common issues

- Recent activity is missing from the associated deal or company, or an overview has not been generated yet.
- The overview is summarizing the company while you expected deal-specific context, or the reverse.
- Source records are duplicated or mismatched.
