Use the Deals board as the starting point for pipeline review and account follow-up. The board reads from Ergo's CRM-backed deal data and can show deal fields, health, activity recency, draft status, reminders, and custom mapped fields.

## Who can use this

- Sales reps, account owners, managers, RevOps, and admins working from pipeline and account context.

## Before you start

- Connect CRM and complete field mapping before relying on CRM writeback.
- Confirm Deals access is enabled for your workspace; contact your admin or Ergo support if you do not see it.
- Choose the correct pipeline or the Companies selector option before searching.
- Clear filters and saved views before assuming a record is missing.

## Steps

- Open **Deals**.
- Use the selector to choose a CRM pipeline or switch to **Companies**.
- Choose **Kanban** to scan stages or **List** to compare fields across records.
- Use built-in views such as **Ergo View**, **Deals**, **This Week**, **Follow-Ups**, and reminders when they are enabled for your workspace.

## What to expect

- The board is scoped by pipeline, view, filters, search, and feature access.
- Card and list fields can include standard deal fields plus mapped custom fields.
- Some actions write through CRM-backed paths; only use them after confirming field mapping and permissions.

## Common issues

- The wrong pipeline, Companies view, built-in tab, or saved custom view is selected.
- Search or filters are still active.
- CRM record names, domains, owners, or associations do not match the context you expected.
- Field mapping or CRM permissions are incomplete.
