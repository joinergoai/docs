Use **Companies** when you want account-level context instead of a single deal pipeline. The company board is selected from the same top selector as pipelines.

## Who can use this

- Sales reps, account owners, managers, RevOps, and admins working from pipeline and account context.

## Before you start

- Connect CRM and complete field mapping before relying on CRM writeback.
- Check pipeline, view, filter, ownership, and record matching before assuming a record is missing.
- Confirm Deals access is enabled for your workspace; contact your admin or Ergo support if you do not see it.

## Steps

- Select **Companies** from the Deals selector.
- Review company cards or list rows for account context.
- Search, sort, and filter by supported company fields.
- Open a company to inspect available activity, emails, meetings, health, AI overview, contacts, and related deals.

## What to expect

- Companies are separate from deal pipeline selection.
- Company fields depend on CRM ingestion and mapped company properties.
- Company-level context may reveal activity that is not attached to the deal you were viewing.

## Common issues

- You are still on a deal pipeline rather than **Companies**.
- Company domains or aliases are incomplete.
- Company custom fields are not mapped.
