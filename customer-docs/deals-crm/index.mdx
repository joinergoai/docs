Use these articles when you are working in the Deals workspace, reviewing account context, or checking why a deal, company, draft, reminder, or CRM update looks different than expected.
## Who this section is for

- Sales reps, account owners, managers, RevOps, and admins working from pipeline and account context.

## Before you start

- Connect the CRM before relying on CRM-backed records. Complete field mapping before relying on CRM writeback of mapped or AI-extracted fields.
- Confirm your workspace has Deals enabled; contact your admin or Ergo support if you do not see it.
- Check the selected pipeline or Companies view, saved view, search, filters, ownership, and matching before treating a record as missing.
- Treat AI summaries, health, drafts, and reminders as review surfaces. They reflect the source activity Ergo has indexed, so verify source activity before making broad CRM changes.
