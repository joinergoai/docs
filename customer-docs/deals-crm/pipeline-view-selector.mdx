Use the selector at the top of Deals to choose a CRM pipeline or the **Companies** option. Pipeline selection and view selection are separate: the pipeline or Companies option controls the source scope, while built-in and saved views control filters and layout.

## Who can use this

- Sales reps, account owners, managers, RevOps, and admins working from pipeline and account context.

## Before you start

- Connect CRM and complete field mapping before relying on CRM writeback.
- Check pipeline, view, filter, ownership, and record matching before assuming a record is missing.
- Confirm Deals access is enabled for your workspace; contact your admin or Ergo support if you do not see it.

## Steps

- Use the top selector to choose a CRM pipeline or **Companies**.
- Use view tabs for **Ergo View**, **Deals**, **This Week**, **Follow-Ups**, reminder-oriented views when enabled, or saved custom views.
- Switch between kanban and list when you need a different layout for the same scope.
- Check URL parameters and active filters before sharing a board state.

## What to expect

- Choosing **Companies** switches from deal-pipeline controls to the company board.
- Built-in views can have stored preferences for board or list layout.
- Custom views can persist filters, view type, hidden stages, and layout choices.

## Common issues

- The selected pipeline does not contain the record.
- The selector is on **Companies**, not a deal pipeline, or the reverse.
- A custom view or built-in tab is applying filters you did not expect.
