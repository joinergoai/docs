![Flow diagram showing how Add Bot to Meeting sends Ergo to an external meeting link.](../images/meetings-add-bot-flow.svg)

Use **Add Bot to Meeting** when the meeting is live, last-minute, or missing from the upcoming calendar list.

## Before you start

- Copy the full Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams meeting URL.
- Confirm the host will admit the bot if the meeting has a waiting room.
- Use this flow for the Ergo bot. Desktop recording is a separate capture mode.

## Add the bot

- Open the dashboard or **Meetings**.
- Click **Add Bot to Meeting**.
- Paste the meeting URL or select an available calendar event if the modal offers one.
- Submit the request and watch the meeting card for joining or waiting-room status.

## What to expect

- Manually added meetings may be stored as unscheduled or ad hoc capture if they were not matched to a calendar event.
- If the meeting is matched to a calendar event, Ergo can use that event context for title, attendees, and follow-up workflows.
- Invalid or unsupported links can fail before the bot joins.

## After the meeting

- Check the meeting record for recording, transcript, and generated outputs.
- If downstream automations did not run automatically for an ad hoc meeting, use [Push notes or meetings to automations](./push-notes-or-meetings-to-automations) where available.
