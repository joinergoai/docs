Manual notes are useful when you want a meeting record without relying on a bot recording.

## Use manual notes when

- The call was not recorded.
- You want to capture customer context from a live conversation.
- You need to add attendees, CRM records, or structured notes by hand.

## What you can do

- Create and edit notes.
- Link notes to a calendar event or CRM record when the UI supports it.
- Share notes through the note sharing flow.
- Push notes to automation workflows where available.

## Caveats

- Manual notes are not the same as a bot transcript.
- AI outputs depend on the text and context you provide.
- Visibility follows note and meeting-note access rules, not every meeting history rule.
