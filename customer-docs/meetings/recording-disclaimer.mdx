The recording disclaimer setting controls whether Ergo sends or displays a fixed recording notice for notetaker meetings.

## Who can manage it

- Super admins can update the org-level recording disclaimer setting.
- Members should ask an admin if the setting needs to change.

## What to check

- Whether the disclaimer is enabled in notetaker settings.
- Whether your org has separate legal or customer-communication requirements.
- Whether the host or meeting platform also announces recording.

## Important caveats

- The setting does not replace your obligation to follow applicable consent laws or company policy.
- The disclaimer is a notice; it does not collect attendee acknowledgment.
- The bot can still require waiting-room admission.
- Recording-disclaimer state is separate from summary-email settings and meeting sharing settings.
