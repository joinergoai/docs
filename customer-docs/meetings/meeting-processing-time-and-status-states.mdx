Do not treat a meeting as one all-or-nothing state. Ergo can have a recording ready while transcript, summary, insights, drafts, or reporting are still pending.

## State types to keep separate

- **Recording state**: whether media was captured and stored.
- **Transcript state**: whether transcript segments are available.
- **Summary state**: whether a meeting summary or generated note is ready.
- **Insight and reporting state**: whether reporting fields or rubric outputs have been computed.
- **Draft state**: whether post-call emails or follow-ups were generated.
- **Desktop processing state**: desktop recordings can show `processing`, `completed`, `completed_no_transcript`, or `failed`.

## What to do while processing

- Wait for the specific output you need instead of retrying everything immediately.
- Refresh the meeting list or detail page after a few minutes.
- For desktop recordings, a processing card can appear before the completed meeting record is ready.
- Use retry only where the UI exposes retry for the failed output.

## When to escalate

- The recording is missing after the bot was admitted and recording.
- The transcript is empty or failed for a meeting with meaningful audio.
- A desktop recording remains failed.
- A required draft or reporting output is missing after the meeting has a transcript.
