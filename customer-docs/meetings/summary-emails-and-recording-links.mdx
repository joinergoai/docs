Use notetaker settings to control whether post-call summary emails are sent and whether those emails include a button to view the recording.

## Settings to check

- **Send post-call summary email** controls whether the user receives summary emails for completed meetings.
- **Include view recording button** controls whether those emails include a recording link when a shareable recording is available.
- Some org-level defaults can be set by admins, but users may still need to verify their own settings.

## What to expect

- Summary email delivery depends on meeting processing, transcript availability, and configured email settings.
- The recording link is a share/view link. It does not bypass meeting visibility, sharing, or password rules.
- Private meetings can omit or revoke share links depending on privacy state.

## Troubleshooting

- If no email arrives, check whether the meeting completed and whether summary emails are enabled.
- If the email has no recording link, check whether the meeting is private, whether the recording exists, and whether the include-link setting is enabled.
- If a recipient cannot open the link, review meeting sharing and access rules.
