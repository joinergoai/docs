Share links let people open a meeting outside the normal internal meeting list. They do not replace internal visibility controls.

## Share a meeting

- Open the meeting detail page.
- Use the share action to generate or copy the meeting link.
- Add a password if the meeting should require one.
- Send the link only to the intended audience.

## Revoke or restrict access

- Use the revoke or unshare action where available to remove the share token.
- Turn on password protection when a link should remain available but restricted.
- Mark the meeting private when internal visibility should also be restricted.

## What shared viewers can see

- Shared meeting data is filtered before it is returned publicly.
- Password-protected meetings require the password before the meeting content is returned.
- Shared viewers may be asked to authenticate depending on the shared-access flow.
