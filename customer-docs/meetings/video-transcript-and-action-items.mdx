Video, transcript, and action items are related outputs, but they can become ready at different times.

## Video

- Bot-recorded meetings can include video playback when the recording is available.
- Desktop recordings and uploaded recordings may have different media behavior depending on source and processing result.
- If a meeting is private, sharing and email links can be affected even when video exists.

## Transcript

- Transcript entries are used for summaries, meeting chat, quotes, and many downstream workflows.
- If the transcript is empty, short, or missing, generated outputs can be incomplete or absent.

## Action items and summaries

- Action items are generated from meeting content after processing.
- Review generated action items before copying them to CRM, sending drafts, or using them in customer follow-up.
