Time-based deletion is an org-level setting for automatically deleting meetings after a configured number of days.

## Who can manage it

- Super admins can enable or disable time-based deletion.
- The retention value must be at least one day.

## Turn it on

- Open the notetaker settings menu from upcoming meetings.
- Toggle **Time-Based Deletion**.
- Enter the number of days to retain meetings.
- Save and verify the displayed setting.

## What to expect

- The setting applies at the organization level.
- Deleted meeting records are removed from meeting history.
- Deletion is separate from notetaker scheduling, sharing, summary emails, and CRM or draft outputs that may already exist.
