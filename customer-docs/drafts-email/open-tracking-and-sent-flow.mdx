Open tracking helps you see whether an email sent through Ergo has detected opens. Treat it as a signal, not a complete delivery or engagement record.

## Who can use this

- Reps reviewing recently sent emails.
- Managers checking sent email activity patterns.

## Before you start

- Send the email through Ergo.
- Wait for sent-state and tracking data to update.
- Confirm open tracking is enabled for your workspace and sender.

## Steps

- Open **Drafts**.
- Go to the **Sent** queue.
- Use tracking filters such as opened, unopened, or opened today.
- Open the recent opens rail or sent-flow view when available.
- Use the sent record to decide whether follow-up is appropriate.

## What to expect

- Sent records can include sent time, pending state, open count, first open, and last open.
- Open tracking can show that an email was opened and when the latest open was detected.
- Open tracking is pixel-based and can be blocked or inflated by email clients, proxies, previews, or security scanners.
- Do not assume tracking identifies the exact recipient who opened an email.
- Scheduled sends and provider updates can lag before sent or open data appears.

## Common issues

- No opens are detected because the recipient's client blocks tracking pixels.
- An internal preview or security scanner causes an open event.
- A scheduled email has not reached final sent-state yet.
- Tracking filters hide the email you expect to see.
