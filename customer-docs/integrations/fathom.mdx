Use this page when Fathom is one of your meeting recording sources.

## Who can use this

- Users and operators configuring Fathom ingestion.
- Admins reviewing third-party notetaker overlap.

## Before you start

- Confirm your workspace has access to the Fathom integration.
- Follow the Fathom-specific instructions shown in Ergo.
- Decide how Fathom should coexist with other notetakers.

## Steps

- Open **Integrations** and select **Fathom**.
- Follow the instructions shown in the Fathom panel.
- Save the required secret or credential in Ergo.
- Test with a known Fathom recording and verify it appears in Ergo.

## What to expect

- Fathom webhook verification depends on the saved secret and supported webhook payloads.
- Ergo may skip a Fathom payload if the recorded-by email cannot be mapped to the expected Ergo user.
- Duplicate-source review is important if another notetaker records the same meeting.

## Common issues

- The webhook secret does not match.
- The recorded-by email is not mapped to the Ergo user.
- The Fathom payload is valid but another notetaker source already captured the meeting.
