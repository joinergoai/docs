Use this page when BlueDot is one of your meeting recording or transcription sources.

## Who can use this

- Users and operators configuring a BlueDot source.
- Admins reviewing third-party notetaker overlap.

## Before you start

- Confirm your workspace has access to the BlueDot integration.
- Have the BlueDot sign-in secret required by the integration panel.
- Decide whether BlueDot should be enabled alongside other notetakers.

## Steps

- Open **Integrations** and select **BlueDot**.
- Enter the BlueDot sign-in secret.
- Confirm BlueDot shows connected.
- Run one test meeting and verify the meeting appears in Ergo.

## What to expect

- BlueDot uses a sign-in secret in Ergo.
- Meeting availability depends on BlueDot sending a supported webhook payload with meeting data.
- If the payload does not include expected attendees, transcript, or summary data, downstream output may be incomplete.

## Common issues

- The sign-in secret is wrong or stale.
- The BlueDot payload was missing required meeting fields.
- Another notetaker source also captured the meeting.
