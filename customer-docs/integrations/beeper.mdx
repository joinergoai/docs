Use this page to connect Beeper when the Beeper integration is enabled for your workspace.

## Who can use this

- Admins and operators enabling messaging context for a workspace.
- Users asked to provide a Beeper token for supported workflows.

## Before you start

- Confirm the Beeper integration is enabled for your workspace.
- Use the token source your admin provided.
- Treat Beeper as a configured messaging source, not as a replacement for Slack or email/calendar connections.

## Steps

- Open **Integrations** and select **Beeper**.
- Paste the Beeper token.
- Confirm the card shows connected.
- Test the workflow your team expects Beeper to power.

## What to expect

- Beeper is token-based and feature-gated.
- Downstream behavior depends on the workflow enabled for your team and the token's access.

## Common issues

- The integration is not enabled for the workspace.
- The token is expired, copied incorrectly, or lacks the expected access.
- The team expected Slack or email behavior from a Beeper-only connection.
