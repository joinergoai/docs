Use this page to connect Attio when Attio is your CRM source for deals, companies, contacts, and fields.

## Who can use this

- Admins and RevOps teams configuring CRM access.
- Operators validating object, field, and pipeline behavior.

## Before you start

- Use an Attio account with access to the workspace records and objects Ergo should use.
- Confirm the relevant Attio lists, attributes, and permissions before relying on CRM updates.
- Review field mapping and CRM matching before enabling automation.

## Steps

- Open **Integrations** and select **Attio**.
- Complete the OAuth flow.
- Confirm Attio appears connected.
- Review field mapping and object matching with your admin.

## What to expect

- Ergo can use Attio CRM data where your workspace has connected and mapped the relevant objects.
- Attio permissions, object setup, and attribute mapping determine which records Ergo can read or update.

## Common issues

- The connected account cannot access the target Attio object or attribute.
- A list or attribute changed after setup.
- A record is missing because the meeting or email was not matched to the expected CRM entity.
