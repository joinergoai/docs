Use this section to connect the external systems that feed Ergo with CRM records, email and calendar context, meeting recordings, Slack context, dialer calls, support handoffs, and external agent access.

## Who this section is for

- Admins and RevOps teams choosing workspace-level sources.
- Users connecting personal sources such as email, calendar, Slack, or a notetaker.

## Before you start

- Know whether the integration is personal, workspace-level, or admin-only.
- Use the account your team expects Ergo to read from or write through.
- Approve every required scope during OAuth and keep API keys, webhook secrets, and signing keys current.
- Verify the downstream workflow after connecting. A connected card does not mean every CRM object, Slack channel, calendar event, or recording is accessible.

## Common issue path

- For expired Google or Microsoft grants, start with [Expired grants and reconnecting](./expired-grants-and-reconnecting).
- For CRM object or field issues, check the CRM integration page and [Field mapping](../field-mapping/index).
- For notetaker overlap, compare the bot, desktop, and third-party notetaker pages before enabling multiple sources.
- If the symptom crosses sources or permissions, use [Troubleshooting](../troubleshooting/index).
