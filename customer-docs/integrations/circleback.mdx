Use this page when Circleback is one of your meeting note sources.

## Who can use this

- Users and operators configuring Circleback automation delivery.
- Admins reviewing third-party notetaker overlap.

## Before you start

- Have access to Circleback automations.
- Copy the webhook URL and sign-in secret shown in Ergo.
- Decide how Circleback should coexist with other notetaker sources.

## Steps

- Open **Integrations** and select **Circleback**.
- Copy the webhook URL shown in Ergo.
- In Circleback, create an automation that sends meeting notes, transcript, and action items to the webhook after meetings.
- Paste the Circleback sign-in secret in Ergo and connect.
- Test with one meeting.

## What to expect

- Circleback delivery depends on the Circleback automation firing after the meeting.
- Ergo processes the payload it receives; missing transcript, notes, or action items in Circleback can limit downstream output.
- Duplicate-source review is important if another notetaker also records the same call.

## Common issues

- The webhook URL was copied incorrectly.
- Circleback automation does not include the expected notes, transcript, or action items.
- A meeting is delivered more than once or another notetaker source already captured it.
