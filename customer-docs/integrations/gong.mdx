Use this page to connect Gong when Gong is one of your meeting recording sources.

## Who can use this

- Admins and operators configuring a Gong-based notetaker source.
- Users validating whether Gong meetings are appearing in Ergo.

## Before you start

- Confirm your workspace has access to the Gong integration.
- Have the credentials or JWT key requested by the integration panel.
- Decide how Gong should coexist with Ergo Notetaker, Ergo Desktop, and other third-party notetakers.

## Steps

- Open **Integrations** and select **Gong**.
- Complete the OAuth or credential flow shown in Ergo.
- If prompted, provide the signed JWT key used for webhook verification.
- Confirm Gong appears connected and test with a known meeting.

## What to expect

- Gong is treated as a notetaker/recording source.
- Connection state may depend on both org-level OAuth and the JWT key required for webhooks.
- Duplicate recordings can happen if another notetaker source captures the same call.

## Common issues

- The JWT key is missing or incorrect.
- Gong is connected but the specific meeting was not delivered or matched.
- Another notetaker source captured the same meeting.
